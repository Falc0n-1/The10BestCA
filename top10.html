Top 10 Places in Canada

Explore the most iconic and beautiful destinations in Canada.

1. Banff National Park, AB

Famous for its surreal turquoise glacial lakes and majestic mountain peaks.

2. Niagara Falls, ON

One of the most powerful and famous waterfalls in the entire world.

3. CN Tower, Toronto, ON

An iconic staple of the Toronto skyline offering incredible city views.

4. Old Quebec, QC

Step back in time with cobblestone streets and European architecture.

5. Whistler, BC

A premier skiing destination and an outdoor adventurer's paradise year-round.

6. Gros Morne, NL

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for dramatic fjords and unique geology.

7. Parliament Hill, Ottawa, ON

The political heart of Canada, featuring stunning Gothic Revival architecture.

8. Bay of Fundy, NB/NS

Home to the highest tides in the world and amazing rock formations.

9. Stanley Park, Vancouver, BC

A massive urban oasis featuring a scenic seawall and ancient cedars.

10. Churchill, MB

The polar bear capital of the world and a great place to see the Northern Lights.
